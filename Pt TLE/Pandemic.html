Pandemic


Without a doubt, the tourism industry is among the sectors that have been greatly affected by the COVID-19 pandemic.
The closing of borders, airports, and hotels as well as restrictions on mass gatherings, land travel and related services
across the world put around 100 to 120 million jobs at risk, as estimated by the World Tourism Organization.


In the first quarter of 2020, the period when the travel restrictions and lockdowns
in most countries started, international tourist arrivals declined by 22% resulting
in an estimated loss of US$80bn in global tourism receipts.
